Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

FileShare Pro ("we", "us", "our") is a peer-to-peer file sharing application developed for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app works.

Zero Server Architecture: FileShare Pro operates on a 100% Peer-to-Peer model. We do not operate any backend servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your files, messages, and personal data never pass through our servers because we have none.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Display Name & Profile Photo: Stored locally on your device only. Not uploaded anywhere.
  • Vault PIN: Your PIN hash is stored locally on your device using SHA-256 + PBKDF2. We cannot recover your PIN.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Advertising Data (via Google AdMob): We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Crash & Performance Data: Standard Android crash reporting may transmit anonymized crash data to help improve the app.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your location data (used only at runtime for device discovery)
  • We do NOT upload your contacts to any server
  • We do NOT store your files on any cloud or server
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT collect or transmit your chat messages

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to serve non-intrusive banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Advertising Policies. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's Google Settings.

4.2 Reddit Public API (Meme Feed)

The Entertainment section fetches publicly available content from Reddit's public JSON API. We do not authenticate with Reddit on your behalf, and no Reddit account data is collected.

4.3 STUN/TURN Servers

For long-distance file transfers, the app uses publicly available STUN servers (Google, Cloudflare) and TURN relay servers to establish peer-to-peer WebRTC connections. These servers facilitate the connection only — your files are transferred directly between devices and are not stored on these relay servers.

4.4 Google Gemini AI

We use the Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini) to provide smart chat reply suggestions and AI-generated file insights. When you use the AI chat features, the last received message or file metadata (such as name and size) is sent to Google's API for processing. We do not store these AI prompts or responses on our servers. For more details, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Ephemeral Stories

The app allows you to create and share 24-hour "Stories" using your camera and media storage. Story media files are saved securely in your device's temporary cache directory and are automatically deleted after 24 hours or when the app clears its cache. We do not upload your stories to any cloud server.

6. User-Generated Content (UGC)

The Entertainment section displays memes and videos sourced from public Reddit feeds. We implement the following safety measures:

  • NSFW Filtering: All content is automatically filtered using keyword-based and subreddit-based NSFW detection. Content flagged as NSFW or containing adult keywords is blocked.
  • Report Feature: Users can report inappropriate content directly from the feed.
  • Block Feature: Users can block specific content authors to hide all their future posts.
Content Disclaimer: We do not create, host, or endorse any user-generated content shown in the feed. All content is sourced from public APIs and is filtered for safety. If you encounter objectionable material, please use the in-app report feature.

6. WhatsApp Status Saver

The Status Saver feature accesses locally stored WhatsApp status files from your device's storage using Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF). Specifically:

  • It reads files from the .Statuses folder on your device only.
  • No WhatsApp data is uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any server.
  • This feature only works with content already downloaded to your device by WhatsApp.

7. Data Security

  • Vault Encryption: Files stored in the vault are encrypted using AES-256-CBC with PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations).
  • Transfer Encryption: Optional PIN-based encryption for file transfers using the same AES-256 standard.
  • Local Storage: All app data (settings, history, chat) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and on-device file storage.
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not backup your data to any cloud service.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data except vault files stored in public Documents storage (these persist for your security).
  • You can manually clear all app data from Settings → Clear All Local Data.
  • You can clear the vault from Settings → Clear Vault.

9. Children's Privacy

FileShare Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The entertainment feed implements NSFW filtering to prevent exposure to inappropriate content.

10. Video Downloading

Important: FileShare Pro does not include any video downloading functionality from YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or any other third-party platform. The app is strictly a peer-to-peer file sharing tool with local encryption capabilities.
