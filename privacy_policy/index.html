Privacy Policy

App: FileShare Pro

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

🚀 Zero Server, Maximum Privacy

FileShare Pro is a completely peer-to-peer (P2P) application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it directly with another user.

1. Information We Access (Device Permissions)

To provide core functionalities, the app requests the following local device permissions. None of this data is collected by us:

  • Camera: Used strictly for scanning QR codes to establish P2P WebRTC connections.
  • Location: Required by the Android OS for Wi-Fi Direct peer discovery (Nearby Share). We do not track your geographical location.
  • Storage / Media: Accessed to allow you to select files to share, save received files, secure files in the local encrypted vault, and fetch temporary WhatsApp statuses.
  • Contacts: Read locally to display your contact list for the in-app chat feature. Contact data is never uploaded to any server.
  • Microphone: Used locally to record voice notes in the chat feature. Audio is sent directly to your peer using End-to-End Encryption.

2. Data Encryption and Security

All communication and file transfers between devices happen directly over local Wi-Fi or WebRTC data channels.

  • End-to-End Encryption (E2E): Every text message, voice note, and file shared through the chat feature is encrypted using AES-256 before transmission.
  • Local Vault: Files stored in the secure vault are encrypted locally on your device. Without your PIN/Biometrics, they cannot be accessed. We do not have your PIN.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google's public STUN servers strictly to help devices discover each other's IP addresses for WebRTC connections. No file data or personal info is sent to Google.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
