Privacy Policy

App: FileShare Pro

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

🚀 Zero Server, Maximum Privacy

FileShare Pro is a completely peer-to-peer (P2P) application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it directly with another user.

1. Information We Access (Device Permissions)

To provide core functionalities, the app requests the following local device permissions. None of this data is collected by us:

  • Camera: Used strictly for scanning QR codes to establish P2P WebRTC connections.
  • Location: We collect location data to enable the discovery of nearby devices for Wi-Fi Direct file transfers, even when the app is closed or not in use. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Storage / Media: Accessed to allow you to select files to share, save received files, secure files in the local encrypted vault, and fetch temporary WhatsApp statuses locally.
  • Contacts: Read locally to display your contact list for the in-app chat feature. Contact data is never uploaded to any server.
  • Microphone: Used locally to record voice notes in the chat feature. Audio is sent directly to your peer using End-to-End Encryption.

2. Features and Data Usage

All communication and file transfers between devices happen directly over local Wi-Fi or WebRTC data channels.

  • End-to-End Encryption (E2E): Every text message, voice note, and file shared through the chat feature is encrypted using AES-256 before transmission.
  • Local Vault: Files stored in the secure vault are encrypted locally on your device. Without your PIN/Biometrics, they cannot be accessed. We do not have your PIN.
  • WhatsApp Status Saver: The app accesses local device directories to display WhatsApp statuses. Disclaimer: This app is not affiliated with, sponsored, or endorsed by WhatsApp Inc. Any unauthorized downloading or re-uploading of contents is the sole responsibility of the user.
  • Video Downloader: The app facilitates downloading videos from various social media platforms directly to your device. Note: Downloading from YouTube is strictly prohibited and blocked within the app to comply with Google Play Policies.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google STUN Servers: Used strictly to help devices discover each other's IP addresses for WebRTC connections. No file data or personal info is sent to Google.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements within the Entertainment section. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can learn more about Google's data practices at Google Privacy Policy. You may opt out of personalized ads through your device's Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Entertainment Feed: The app fetches publicly available meme content from third-party APIs. This content is filtered for safety but is not created or controlled by us. Users can report or block inappropriate content directly within the app.

4. User-Generated Content (UGC) & Safety

FileShare Pro provides features like in-app chat and file sharing. To ensure a safe environment, we adhere to strict User-Generated Content (UGC) policies:

  • Zero Tolerance: We have a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content or abusive users. Sharing illegal, abusive, or sexually explicit content is strictly prohibited.
  • Block & Report: Users have the ability to instantly Block abusive peers and Report inappropriate content directly within the app.
  • Moderation: While all data is encrypted and P2P, users found violating our terms through verifiable reports may be permanently banned from using the app's communication features.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
